**An AI-Powered Academic Ecosystem for PESMCOE Autonomous Curriculum**

PES-Nexus is a centralized platform designed to bridge the gap between students,seniors and syllabus. It streamlines learning by providing AI-driven insights, official resources, and a gamified peer-mentorship system.

## üöÄ Key Features
* **Syllabus Explorer:** Department-wise breakdown of the autonomous curriculum with unit-level details.
* **Nexus AI Tutor:** A Google Gemini-powered assistant providing grounded explanations and curated resources (Books/YouTube).
* **Senior Bridge:** A real-time doubt-solving platform connecting students with experienced seniors.
* **Gamified Leaderboard:** Rewards seniors with "Nexus Points" for helping peers, fostering a collaborative community.
* **Official Resources:** Integrated access to Official Syllabus PDFs and Previous Year Question Papers (PYQs).
* **Faculty Analytics:** Tracking trending doubt topics to help teachers focus on difficult concepts.
